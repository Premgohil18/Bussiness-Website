MAIZE HOMINY FEED

Jyoti Cornex’s hominy meal is pre-grounded mixture of corn germ, corn grits, high fat flour, extraneous corn meal, bran and screenings. Sold under the branding of Cornex Cattle Nutrion, this hominy meal is a unique feed product that combines superior feed value with competitive pricing when compared to the traditional feed corn.

  • Crude Protein – consistently 25%-30% higher than feed corn
  • Crude Fat – consistently 100% higher than feed corn.
  • Essential Amino Acids – notably higher levels of lysine & tryptophan
  • Minerals – content is significantly higher across the board
  • Ready to use – no labour and energy costs like with grinding corn
