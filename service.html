Export

We are one of the top-notch Exporters and Suppliers of Corn Grits from Maharashtra, India. The Reason behind the high demand of our Corn Grits globally is its 100% purity, high nutritional content and longer shelf life. We process Corn Grits under the most hygienic conditions supervised by our team of experts. Buyers can obtain Corn Grits from us at the most competitive prices.

With focus on stringent quality & customer satisfaction Jyoti Cornex Industries is a recognized name in the global arena with customers in 21+ countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar , Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, Jordan, Iraq, Mauritius, Lebanon, Fiji, Liberia, Angola, Brunei, Israel, Indonesia, Korea, Yemen, Bangladesh, Sudan etc. With its products being well accepted across the globe, Jyoti Cornex Industries have gained a strong foothold in these markets. .
